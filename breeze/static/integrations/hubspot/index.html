Hi Energy AI — HubSpot setup guide

Connect Hi Energy affiliate intelligence to HubSpot CRM with OAuth install and an API key.

Overview

  • Company records — Research advertisers and deals by domain or company name
  • Contact records — Search advertisers, deals, and contacts from email or company
  • Research company — One-click research from the company record sidebar

Hi Energy data is fetched server-side with your API key and is not written into HubSpot CRM properties by default.

Requirements

  • Permission to install Marketplace apps in HubSpot
  • Active Hi Energy AI subscription with API access
  • Hi Energy API key — create one in Hi Energy

Install the app

  1. Open the Hi Energy AI listing on the HubSpot Marketplace.
  2. Click Install app and choose your HubSpot account.
  3. Review OAuth scopes and click Connect app.
ScopeWhy Hi Energy AI needs it
crm.objects.companies.read Read company domain and name to research advertisers
crm.objects.contacts.read Read contact email and company to run Hi Energy search

Connect your Hi Energy API key

  1. In HubSpot, open Connected apps.
  2. Click Hi Energy AI.
  3. Open the Settings tab.
  4. Paste your Hi Energy API key and click Save and test connection.

Need help? Email support@hienergy.ai.

Add CRM cards

App cards are not added automatically. After install, add them from the record customize screen.

Company tab — Hi Energy company research

  1. Open a company record → Customize → Default view.
  2. In Card library, filter App and add Hi Energy company research.

Company sidebar — Research company

  1. On the same customize screen, open the right sidebar Card library.
  2. Add Research company.

Contact tab — Hi Energy contact search

  1. Open a contact record → Customize → Default view.
  2. Add Hi Energy contact search from the App card library.

Troubleshooting

IssueWhat to do
Cards ask for an API key Add your key in Connected apps → Hi Energy AI → Settings.
No matches Confirm company domain or contact email/company is populated in HubSpot.
Cards missing Add app cards through Customize → Card library → App.
